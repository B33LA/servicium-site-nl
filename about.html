FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with both businesses and individuals? +

Yes! We work with sole traders (eenmanszaak), BVs, freelancers, and private individuals — including expats at all stages of their Dutch financial journey.

What is the 30% ruling and do I qualify? +

The 30% ruling is a Dutch tax advantage for highly skilled migrants, allowing 30% of your salary to be paid tax-free. Eligibility depends on salary level, role, and residency history. We assess your situation and handle the application end-to-end.

How quickly do you respond to queries? +

We aim to respond to all client emails within one business day. For urgent matters, we always make time to help — our clients frequently tell us our responsiveness is one of our best qualities.

What software do you use? +

We use modern cloud-based accounting tools including Exact Online, Moneybird, and others depending on your needs. We ensure seamless integration with your existing systems.

Is the first consultation really free? +

Absolutely — no strings attached. The first 30-minute call is completely free. We use it to understand your situation and see if we're the right fit. Book directly via our calendar link.
